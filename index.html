Olivia Wiles

I work as a Research Scientist at DeepMind, focussing on robustness of various forms: robustness to distribution shift and robustness to adversarial examples.

I was a DPhil student at the University of Oxford in the visual geometry group (VGG) supervised by Professor Andrew Zisserman. Before that, I studied computer science at the University of Cambridge.

I also spent a wonderful summer working with Georgia Gkioxari, Justin Johnson, and Richard Szeliski at Facebook AI Research (FAIR).
